About Mt. Fuji

Mt. Fuji is a famous volcanic mountain located in Japan. It stands at 3,776 meters (12,388 feet) tall and is known for its perfectly symmetrical cone shape. The mountain is an important cultural and natural landmark in Japan, and it has been a popular destination for hikers and tourists for centuries.
